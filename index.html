What is MultiVox?

MultiVox is a lightweight Windows desktop app that lets multiple Bluetooth devices connect to a single PC simultaneously. It captures your PC's audio output and wirelessly repeats it to every connected Bluetooth headphone, earbud, or speaker — in real time, with individual volume control for each device. No extra hardware. No complicated setup. Just open the app, connect your devices, and everyone hears the same thing.

Powerful Features

Multi-Device Output

Send your PC's audio to multiple Bluetooth headphones, earbuds, or speakers simultaneously.

Volume Control

Each device gets its own volume slider. Everyone controls their own level independently.

BT Manager

Connect, disconnect, and manage paired devices right inside the app.

Live Monitoring

Visual level meters show input and output levels per device in real time.

One-Click Start

Hit START and audio flows to all devices. No configuration needed.

Private & Offline

Fully offline — no accounts, no cloud, no data collection. Pure local audio.
